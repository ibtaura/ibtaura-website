Investor & Partner FAQ.

There is no dedicated destination for wearable health monitoring systems. You have Apple and Samsung for gadgets. GE Healthcare and Siemens for medical equipment. But nobody owns the wearable health monitoring space. We are building that place. Our first product bridges the gap between medical grade accuracy and everyday comfort. Because right now, products are either too clinical to wear daily or too casual to trust medically. We are solving both at once.

Simple and proven. A one time payment for the hardware and a subscription for our health and wellness application. Two revenue streams from day one.

We are in the premium health market, serving health enthusiasts, fitness focused individuals and people who take their wellbeing seriously. We are launching in the United States first, then expanding to India, Singapore and Europe.

They are tech products. We are a health company. Our ecosystem is built around genuine health outcomes and spiritual wellness, not just notifications and step counts. Every product we build connects seamlessly into one ecosystem that addresses both physical and spiritual health. That is something no one else is building.

We are in the final stages of product development. Clinical trials are next. We are on track to launch by July 2026.

Because you are looking at the next defining company in human health. The way Apple defined personal technology, we are defining personal health. This is innovation meeting real human problems at the right time. The wearable health market is wide open and we are building the ecosystem that will own it.

IBT Aura was founded by a small, focused team of people who believe deeply in the mission. We bring together experience across health technology, product design, and business operations. We are builders first, not promoters. Every person on this team is here because they believe in what we are creating, not just what it could be worth.

The global wearable health market is projected to exceed $380 billion by 2030. Within that, the premium segment — where we operate — is the fastest growing. Beyond wearables, the broader digital health and wellness industry represents a multi-trillion dollar shift in how humans take care of themselves. We are building at the intersection of all of it.

We launch direct-to-consumer in the United States first, followed by India and Singapore. Our strategy is community-led — we will build a loyal base of early adopters who believe in the mission before we scale paid acquisition. Healthcare partnerships and corporate wellness programs form our second channel. We grow with trust, not just spend.

User privacy is non-negotiable. Health data is stored with end-to-end encryption, processed on-device wherever possible, and never sold to third parties. We are HIPAA-compliant by design, not as an afterthought. We believe your health data belongs to you — and our entire architecture is built around that principle.

The main risks are hardware development timelines, regulatory approvals, and market adoption. We mitigate these by maintaining lean operations, working with experienced manufacturing partners, and pursuing regulatory compliance from day one rather than retrofitting it later. Our subscription model reduces our reliance on hardware margin alone, giving us financial resilience across cycles.

Reach out to us directly at lagaura.info@gmail.com. We respond to every genuine inquiry. You can also download our pitch deck for a structured overview of our business, traction, and financial projections. We look forward to the conversation.
