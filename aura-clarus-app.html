Travel

Stay on top of your health wherever you go. Track jet lag recovery, monitor your body clock adjustments, and get real-time guidance for maintaining wellness across time zones and travel schedules.

Smart Calendar

Your schedule, optimised by your body. Meetings, tasks, and breaks are intelligently arranged around your health data — helping you perform at your best, every hour of the day.

Goals

Set personal wellness targets and watch your progress unfold over time. Define what matters most — steps, sleep, heart health, or mindfulness — and let the app guide you with daily check-ins and visual progress tracking.

Story

Your wellness journey told through a visual daily narrative. Review patterns, celebrate milestones, and understand how your choices shape your health — all presented in a timeline that makes your progress feel personal and meaningful.

Actions

Receive intelligent, actionable steps tailored to your body. Based on your real-time vitals and daily trends, the app suggests what to do next — whether it is a breathing exercise, a hydration reminder, or the right moment to move.

Streak

Build consistency with daily health monitoring and turn wellness into a habit. Track your streak across ECG readings, activity goals, and check-ins — the longer you stay consistent, the deeper your health insights become.

Fitness

Monitor your workouts with real-time pace, distance, heart rate, and performance insights. Whether you are running, walking, or training, the app tracks every session and shows you exactly how your body responded.

Log

Track and record your daily health entries with precision. Log meals, symptoms, mood, medication, and vitals — everything in one place, organised chronologically so you and your doctor always have the full picture.

Scores

A single score that captures your complete wellness picture. Combining heart health, activity, sleep quality, and recovery into one easy-to-read number — so you always know where you stand and what to improve.

Parameters

View all your key health metrics in one place. Heart rate, blood oxygen, respiratory rate, body temperature, and more — tracked continuously and presented with clarity so you always have the full picture of your vitals.
